Few Words About Oya Kakers

At Oya Kakers, we are passionate about crafting moments of warmth, joy, and indulgence for every customer who walks through our doors. Since our humble beginnings in 2017, we have been a renowned European cake brand with multiple outlets, delighting dessert lovers with our premium creations.

What sets OYA Kekars apart is our commitment to an exceptional experience. From our live kitchen, where you can witness the artistry and dedication behind every cake, to our unwavering focus on maintaining the highest hygiene standards, we go above and beyond to ensure that every bite is nothing short of perfection

Our mousse cakes are a signature delight at OYA Kekars, crafted to perfection with premium ingredients and a smooth, airy texture that melts in your mouth. Prepared fresh in our live kitchen with uncompromising hygiene standards, our mousse cakes are the perfect choice for every celebration, offering a balance of luxury, taste, and sophistication. Innovation is at the core of our philosophy. Our uniquely developed software provides real-time information about the consumption period of our cakes, ensuring not only the freshest flavors but also the safety and well-being of our valued customers.

Our Philosophy

‘O’ means calling someone with respect and ‘YA’ refers to welcoming someone. At OYA Kekars, we welcome our customers with loving hearts and open arms.

Our mission is to craft not just cakes but exceptional experiences that bring joy and delight to every customer. Our state-of-the-art software ensures that every cake is enjoyed at its freshest, precisely tracking its consumable period for your peace of mind.

Transparency and trust are at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

At OYA Kekars, our mission is to redefine the cake-buying experience with a strong focus on hygiene, safety, and customer satisfaction.

We are dedicated to offering contactless service, ensuring every interaction prioritizes your health and well-being.

As we continue to innovate and expand, we aim to bring our premium desserts and unparalleled service to more communities.

Our Vision
